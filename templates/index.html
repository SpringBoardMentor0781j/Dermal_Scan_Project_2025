DermalScan

AI-Powered Facial Skin Aging Classifier and Age/Gender Predictor

How to Use:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to select an image from your device.
  2. Ensure the image is in .jpg, .jpeg, or .png format.
  3. The face should be clearly visible and well-lit for best results.
  4. Click "Analyze Image" and wait for the results.

Developed with Flask and TensorFlow
